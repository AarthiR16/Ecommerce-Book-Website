About Us

Bookish Open Door is a charitable nonprofit organization created by BookishMedia in support of adult and young adult literacy. While Bookish focuses on memoir, travel and young–adult fiction with a consistent theme of crossing cultures, Bookish Open Door books address the under-served population of people who struggle to read. Together, they make a powerful literary combination to encourage reading.
